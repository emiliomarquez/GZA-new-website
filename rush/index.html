Rush Gammas 2021

Becoming a brother of Gamma Zeta Alpha Fraternity, Inc. Nu Chapter is a lifelong commitment; Brothers of Gamma Zeta Alpha Fraternity, Inc. are not only brothers during their academic tenure but for life. The rush process is designed to help potential new members decide if Gamma Zeta Alpha is for them, and to help us determine if a potential new member is able to uphold our values and positively contribute to the fraternity.

Currently, rush is scheduled to start during Spring Quarter 2021.
