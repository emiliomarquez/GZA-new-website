Rush Gammas 2021

Becoming a member of Gamma Zeta Alpha Fraternity, Inc. Nu Chapter is a lifelong commitment; Members of Gamma Zeta Alpha Fraternity, Inc. are not only members during their academic tenure but for life. The rush process is designed to help potential new members decide if Gamma Zeta Alpha is for them, and to help us determine if a potential new member is able to uphold our values and positively contribute to the fraternity. Currently, rush is postponed until 2021 due to the COVID-19 pandemic. Stay tuned for more details as this page and our social media will be updated with upcoming rush events when we can safely meet in-person again.
