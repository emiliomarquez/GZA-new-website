Gamma Zeta Alpha Fraternity, Inc. Nu Chapter Scholarship

PURPOSE

Gamma Zeta Alpha strongly believes in the importance of Academic Success. The purpose of this Scholarship is to reward well-rounded Latinx scholars.

REQUIREMENTS

We are looking for individuals who reflect our 3 principles: Academic Excellence, Community Service, and Maintenance of the Latino Culture through Brotherhood.

To be considered for the Gamma Zeta Alpha Fraternity, Inc. Nu Chapter Scholarship the applicant must meet the following requirements:

  • 3.0 minimum unweighted GPA.
  • Will be attending University of California, San Diego.
  • Will be a full-time student at UCSD (minimum 12 units).
  • Community service hours (if applicable).
  • Letter of recommendation “optional”.

APPLICATION PROCESS
